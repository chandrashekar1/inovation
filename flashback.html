About Maker Mela

Maker Mela is the premier event for grassroot Innovations in India. It is the celebration of arts, crafts, engineering, science, the Do-It-Yourself (DIY) mindset, and the Maker Movement. It's a Platform for like-minded folks who bring to life a diverse range of raw, unique and unconventional ideas.

From pottery to 3D printing, there's some for everyone. You think you have it in you? Come take a quick peek, and meet the maker in you. Get ready to inspire, get inspired, and explore what tickles your curiosity. COME. CREATE. BAKE. MAKE. SHOWCASE.

dr. ravi grover

Ravi B. Grover is Homi Bhabha Chair, Department of Atomic Energy (DAE) and Homi Bhabha National Institute (HBNI), Mumbai, India. Dr. Grover is a member of the Indian Atomic Energy Commission, a Fellow of the Indian National Academy of Engineering, a Fellow of the World Academy of Art and Science and represents India in the ITER Council as chair of the Indian delegation. He was the Vice-Chancellor of HBNI until February 2016. For the first 25 years of his career, Dr. Grover worked as a research and development engineer in the Bhabha Atomic Research Centre (BARC) specializing in nuclear reactor thermal hydraulics and safety studies. He was responsible for the thermal-hydraulic design of the core and the fuel of the 100 MW research reactor Dhruva and led a team of engineers for evolving process design of primary systems, dynamic analysis of the plant systems, and fuel and core thermal hydraulics analysis for a compact reactor. Subsequently, he took up managerial responsibilities and played a key role in opening up international civil nuclear trade with India including negotiations leading to India joining the ITER venture. He played a lead role in the conceptualization and the setting up of HBNI and led the Institute since its accreditation as a deemed to be University in June 2005, and until end February 2016.
